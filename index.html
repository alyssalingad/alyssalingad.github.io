WHO AM I?

I'm an upcoming 3rd year computer science major at University of California, Irvine. In my free time, I take delight in learning about new technologies, playing League of Legends when I should be sleeping, and editing this very website until it fits my standards of the day. I'm interested in network systems and internet security.


I like to look at code from different perspectives and find ways to break it. I'm observant, analytical, and I strive for near perfection. My goal is to make meaningful impact in whatever way I can.
